Introduction

It's the moment all tech-savvy members of society go through.

The computer rig you've set up to be future-proof inexplicably slows down to a crawl from the beast it o nce was.

Frustration runs through your veins, until the consumer inside you kicks in.

Get your kidneys ready. It's time for an upgrade!

The Birth of a New Machine

Where should you start? you have so many decisions to make!

This is your new baby. Every choice matters!

After all, you'll be spending your every online minute with it, atleast for the next few years (we hope!)

Should you sacrifice performance for portability? Or perhaps settle down with a powerful build in a giant ATX tower?

Bigger isn't always better

But what if you want the best of both worlds? What if you want a high-performing machine that's also compact?

Surely the latest and greatest Aero 15X laptop ($2200 USD) by Gigabyte is the obvious solution. Right?

Look no further, there's a new solution on the market.

A mini-itx build that won't break the bank on all the fluff expenses of a good laptop. And still be portable enough to carry in your bag.

I know what you're thinking.

"A mini-itx case is still like the size of microwave. And it's more expensive than a traditional case."

Forget everything you thought, or think you knew, about mini-itx cases. Because you've never seen one like this.
