Frequently Asked Questions

  • "when did you start playing league of legends?"

    Middle of Season 3, I think. Around the time Lissandra was first released!

  • "why did you decide to main lissandra?"

    Mainly because she was the first champion I bought. Lissandra was the first new champion to be release since I first started playing. I thought, if I was going to main a champion it might as well be the latest and greatest that Riot Games has to offer... little did I know I would become addicted to only playing her.

  • "What was your highest rank? Are you really the best Lissandra?"

    I peaked at Diamond 1 in Season 6. I've been playing League on a Mac! According to the op.gg I was Rank 1 Lissandra in the world at one point, but that's more of a bragging-rights achievement than anything. I'm definitely nowhere near that now!

  • "How did you come up with the name 'Elsa of Garendel'? It's weird."

    My friend changed it for me waaay back (I had a weirder name before that!). But I just let it go, since the past is in the past. "Elsa" because Lissandra uses ice. "Garendel" because "Arendelle" passes the character limit. Read more about the origin of Elsa of Garendel.

  • "Okay I have to ask, are you a guy or a girl?"

    All will be revealed in time. Patience young grasshopper.
