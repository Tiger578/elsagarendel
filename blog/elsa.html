Elsa of Garendel: origin

I began playing League of Legends (LoL) in the middle of Season 3, around the time Lissandra first came out. My friends got me into it back in highschool, and I quickly became addicted to the game.

Apart from Maplestory, LoL is the only other "big" game I've played. Everything about it was so new to me, and so exciting. And so my 4 year journey as a League of Legends player began.

Leveling up to 30 was a grind, as many of you may recall when first starting the game. I went through the cycle of Free-to-Play champions week by week, but none of them really seemed that interesting to me. There certainly wasn't as much diversity in the champion pool as there is now. So naturally, I eventually set my eyes on the newest champion that Riot released at the time. Lissandra.

She felt like a breath of fresh air in the mundane grind to level 30. I quickly fell in love with the champion. Her crowd control, her splash damage, her unique gap closer and her reliable abilities, everything about her kit seemed perfect to me. I became so absorbed in her that I more or less stopped playing other champions!

A few months after the movie Frozen was released, a friend of mine suggested I change my name to "Elsa of Arendelle" (forgive us, we were kids!). Since that surpassed the 16-character limit, I shortened it to "Elsa of Garendel". And that's who I've been ever since then!
