About the Site

This site was created for the final project of the MIT 2570 course at The University of Western Ontario. The site's main audience is the Facebook followers and Youtube subscribers, as well as new viewers who watch my League of Legends content on Youtube. The goal is to strengthen the Elsa of Garendel brand, as well as increase subscriber growth from Youtube by 5% for new future uploads. I will be using this site as an extension of my Youtube channel, to publish and promote extra content that doesn't make it into the videos.
